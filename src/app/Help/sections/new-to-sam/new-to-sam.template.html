Who Uses SAM?

Anyone looking to do business with or receive assistance from the federal government should use SAM.

SAM users can generally be divided into three groups:

  • People who make awards - Government contracting and grants officials responsible for activities with contracts, grants, past performance reporting and suspension and debarment activities. These include federal assistance coordinators and agency program managers, contracting officers (COR, contract specialists, procurement analysts), and compliance administrators;
  • People who receive awards - Contractors, federal assistance recipients, and other potential award recipients who need to register to do business with the government, look for opportunities or assistance programs, or report subcontract information;
  • People who track awards - Public users searching for government business information. These include congressional researchers, the Small Business Administration (SBA) and the media.
