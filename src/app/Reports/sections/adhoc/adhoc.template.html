Reports

Sam.gov reports can be used to support analysis of federal spending including geographical analysis, market analysis, and analysis on the imapct of the congressional and presedential initiatives in socio-economic areas such as small business spending. The data is also a reliable basis for measuring and assessing the impact of Federal acquisition policy and management improvement.

Adhoc Reports

The Ad Hoc Reports feature offers the ability to select individual fields from the FPDS database to run a query. Please note that the ideal users of the Ad Hoc Reports feature have both knowledge of the FPDS data fields and understand how to use an advanced reporting tool.
